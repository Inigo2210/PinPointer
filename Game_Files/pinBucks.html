What are Pin Bucks?

  • Pin Bucks are the in-game currency for the Pin Pointer Game. You are given 150 Pin Bucks for free

  • Each time you complete a full 10 rounds of any game mode, your final score plus your time bonus will be added to your Pin Bucks balance

  • Your time bonus is the remaining time on the clock after all correct guesses

  • Negative final scores will result in no change to your Pin Bucks balance

  • Pin Bucks can be used on Power-Ups during rounds to assist you in your guess

  • Some Power-Ups include giving you a different image/flag for the round, stopping the timer, eliminating possible choices, and providing hints

  • Expert and Nightmare modes will give double Pin Bucks for all final scores

  • Broswer cookies MUST be enabled for Pin Bucks to function
